ModelBuilder Project: Site Selection for Wastewater Treatment Plant

This project was developed as part of the Esri course on Building Geoprocessing Models Using ArcGIS Pro.

Key Objective:

Automate site selection for a new wastewater treatment plant using multiple spatial criteria.

Site Selection Criteria:

  • Within 3,000 feet of the Cache la Poudre River
  • Within 1 mile of the city limits
  • At least 300 feet from residential parcels and parks
  • Located outside the floodplain
  • Located on vacant parcels that are 50,000 sq ft or greater

Workflow Using ModelBuilder:

  • Buffered river and city boundary layers
  • Generated exclusion zones for residential areas and parks
  • Used spatial queries to remove parcels within floodplains
  • Selected only vacant parcels ≥ 50,000 sq ft
