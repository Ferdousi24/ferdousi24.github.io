Project Description

This project focuses on mapping and analyzing public facilities in the City of Ottawa, specifically by exploring the functions of QGIS. This work is inspired by the recent completion of a course on QGIS. .

Key Highlights:

  • Data was collected from the City of Ottawa Open Data.
  • The initial part was the foundational step of displaying public facilities on a map
    • Spatial analysis was conducted to Count Fire Stations Using QGIS with Buffering and Joining Techniques within the mapped area.
